Islamic Rituals

Ramadan and Eid Celebration

Ramadan is a holy month where Muslims fast, pray, and help others.

Ramadan

Ramadan is the ninth month in the Islamic calendar. Muslims fast from sunrise to sunset. It is a time for prayer, charity, and reading the Quran.

Eid Al-Fitr

Eid Al-Fitr is the celebration after Ramadan. Muslims pray the Eid prayer, visit family, and share happiness together.

Important Islamic Rituals

  • Prayer (Salah)
  • Fasting in Ramadan
  • Giving Charity (Zakat)
  • Reading Quran
